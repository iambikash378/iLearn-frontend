Ethics in AI

As AI systems become more powerful and pervasive, ethical concerns have taken center stage in the discussion of their development and deployment.

Key ethical issues include:

  • Bias and Fairness: AI can perpetuate or even amplify human biases if not properly addressed.
  • Privacy: Data used to train AI must be handled securely and responsibly.
  • Transparency: Users should understand how AI systems make decisions.
  • Accountability: Developers must take responsibility for the consequences of AI behavior.

Ethical AI requires collaboration between technologists, policymakers, and the public to ensure that the benefits of AI are shared broadly and fairly.
